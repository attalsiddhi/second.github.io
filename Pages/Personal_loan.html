Personal Loan

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that individuals can use for various purposes, such as debt consolidation, home improvement, or unexpected expenses, typically with a fixed interest rate and repayment term.

Get Started

Features and Benefits of Personal Loan

  1. Unsecured: No collateral is required to obtain a personal loan.
  2. Versatile Use: Can be used for a wide range of purposes.
  3. Fixed Interest Rate: Offers predictability with a constant interest rate.
  4. Fixed Repayment Term: Provides a set period for repayment, aiding budgeting.
  5. Quick Approval: Typically has a faster approval process than other loans.
  6. Credit Improvement: Responsible repayment can enhance your credit score.

Personal Loan Eligibility Criteria

  1. Age: Usually, you must be at least 18 years old to apply for a personal loan.
  2. Income: Lenders may require a minimum income to ensure your ability to repay the loan.
  3. Employment: A stable job or a regular source of income is often a prerequisite.
  4. Credit Score: A good credit history and score can improve your chances of approval.
  5. Citizenship/Residency: You may need to be a citizen or resident of the country where you're applying for the loan.
  6. Documents: Proof of identity, address, and income, such as ID, bank statements, and pay stubs, may be necessary.
  7. Debt-to-Income Ratio: Lenders assess your existing debt obligations in relation to your income.

Documents required to apply for Personal Loan

  • Identity Proof - Passport, Voter's ID, Driving License, PAN Card, Aadhaar Card
  • Proof of Residence or Address Proof - Passport, Voter's ID, Driving License, PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Electricity Bill, Telephone Bill, Ration Card
  • Age Proof - Passport, Voter's ID, Driving License, PAN Card, Aadhaar Card
  • Income Proof - 1 year Bank statement, 3 months Salary Slips
  • Employment Proof - Employment Certificate, Office address proof
  • GST or VAT Registration for Self - employed
  • Photograph - Passport-size photographs
  • Business proof - Business registration documents such as a partnership deed, Memorandum of Association (MOA), Articles of Association (AOA), etc.
  • Income tax returns - Documents of the past 2-3 years to verify income and tax payment history
